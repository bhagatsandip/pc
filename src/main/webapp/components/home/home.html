Super Flexibility

You just buy a Planet Clubs membership and instantly gain access to several health and fitness related facilities across locations. Your membership gives you the freedom to switch clubs every month if you wish to. So, you may go to a gym for a few days and then decide on swimming classes for the next month, and so on.

Bonus Free Usage Privileges (FUPs)

Your membership gives you complimentary Free Usage Privileges (FUPs), with each enrollment. These give you access to thousands of add-on services which include pay-as-you-go access to most health clubs, sports facilities, parlors and resorts.

Facilities Galore

PlanetClubs's partnerships cover gyms, health clubs, swimming pools, group classes like aerobics or yoga, tennis clubs, martial art centers, and even spas. And all these, spread across the country. While the membership is valid for one year, the FUPs never expire- if you have FUPs left over, they will remain until you use them or the world ends (whichever comes first).

Find clubs and facilities around you

No matter where you are, if there is a PlanetClubs partner facility around, you will find it. The location-aware app shows which ones are around you. You can also search by city, locality, or type of facility. You may then enroll or use FUPs to use any of the thousands of options across the nation (and very soon, beyond).

How It Works

The PlanetClubs platform has been designed with user convenience, flexibility and simplicity in mind. The way it works is very simple, while the value it offers is enormous.

  • Step 1: Download app

    Download and install the PlanetClubs app from Google Play Store. This is a very simple app that does all the heavy lifting of looking for clubs, clocking your times, and managing your membership with us.

  • Step 2: Buy a membership

    We offer several options- monthly, quarterly, half yearly or annual, across three categories of clubs- Silver, Gold and Platinum. Look at your preferences, clubs around your area and your budget, to decide on your category.

  • Step 3: Enroll with a club

    Locate a club or a facility to use. You may do so by a variety of filters, including location, and type of facility (gym, karate center, yoga, resort etc.). Once you go through all that is on offer, decide on any one of them. And Enroll with them on their respective Club screen, in the app. Your enrollment gives you certain bonus Free Usage Privileges (FUPs) too.

  • Step 4: Check-in to the club

    Just walk over to the partner club where you enrolled, and scan the QR code with your phone. This will record the fact that you have started to use the facility. Alternatively, you may also select it manually from the list. If you walk into a club that is different from the one you enrolled in, then you will have to use your FUPs.

  • Step 5: Club approves

    The club needs to approve your check-in. Once done, your attendance is recorded. If you had checked-in using FUPs, the corresponding FUPs are deducted from your account and transferred to the club.

  • Step 6: Brag about it!

    We encourage you to share your experience with others, using the simple social sharing option in your app. Let the world know that you are doing something good…no, great.
