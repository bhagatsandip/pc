About Us

PlanetClubs was conceived from a real problem faced by its founders themselves- that of being tied to a single fitness provider even when situations changed. A craving to tear apart such restrictions gave rise to the concept, which was then designed and developed by actively involving market players like fitness experts, gyms and gym goers. The team that built and currently maintains this platform is a unique mix of wisdom, experience, energy and expertise. Our motto is to unify fitness related service providers across geographies, and to simplify their access for clients. Our service partners also benefit from our economies of scale, and branding.
